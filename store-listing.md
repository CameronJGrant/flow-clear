## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Light the fuse. Watch it burn. Clear the board.

Flow Clear is a minimalist puzzle game where you tap glowing nodes to ignite fuses that burn along colorful paths. The catch? Some paths are blocked by others — tap in the wrong order and you'll lose a life.

Read the board. Find the unblocked path. Clear it first to free the rest. Simple to learn, deeply satisfying to master.

FEATURES:
• Infinite procedurally generated levels — no two boards are the same
• Intuitive one-tap gameplay with surprising strategic depth
• Satisfying fuse-burn animations and crackling sound effects
• Hand-crafted tutorial that teaches you as you play
• Dark and light mode
• No ads. No paywalls. Just puzzles.

HOW TO PLAY:
1. Tap a glowing node to light its fuse
2. The fuse burns along the path, clearing it from the board
3. Some paths are blocked — their fuse crosses another node
4. Clear the blocking path first to unblock it
5. Lose a life for each wrong tap. Lose all three and retry.
6. Clear all paths to complete the level

From Synapop Studio — made for puzzle lovers who appreciate clean design and satisfying mechanics.
